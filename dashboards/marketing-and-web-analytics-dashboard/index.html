Marketing and Web Analytics Dashboard

Dashboard with KPIs for lead generation, lead sources, lead conversion, click-through rate, sign-up conversions, retention, and social media analytics.

Plotly basics

  • Plotly is free for unlimited public data, or $20 per month for private data: Plans.
  • Plotly charts are made in Python, R, or by uploading Excel/CSV sheets to Plotly's Web App.

Tips for professional dashboards

  • We recommend these free dashboard templates and hosting dashboards on AWS.
  • Plotly charts can also be organized into folders and shared as reports.
  • We recommend Color Brewer for choosing chart colors.
  • Keep your charts simple and don't overclutter.
