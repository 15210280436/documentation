Plotly MATLAB Library

Publish your MATLAB figures to the web with one line!


fig = figure;

% create your mesh grid
[X,Y] = meshgrid(-8:.5:8);
R = sqrt(X.^2 + Y.^2) + eps;
Z = sin(R)./R;

% create your 3D surface
surf(Z);


%--Graph the MATLAB figure online in Plotly!--%
p = fig2plotly(fig);
